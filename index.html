Vocifery is a question answering (QA) engine. It will parse questions and send queries to different content sources on the Internet. The engine then performs semantic analysis on search results to find candidate answers.

Note that this application is still at the "Hello World" stage. A crowdfunding effort is underway to host the QA engine on more powerful servers.

Example Questions
  • Where can I find good barbecue in Kauai?
  • Where can I find hackathon sessions around San Francisco, CA?
  • What caused the Indonesian stock market to halt trading?
  • What causes cancer to metastasize?
  • What is the meaning of life, the universe, and everything?
  • Who assassinated Julius Caesar?
  • Who betrayed Jesus in the gospels?
  • When was Abraham Lincoln assassinated?
  • When does Diwali occur?
  • When did Kate Middleton get married?
