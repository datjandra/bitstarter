Description

Vocifery is a question answering (QA) engine. Questions are parsed for context, and then queries are sent to various Internet search engines. Search results are further analyzed for candidate answers that best satisfy the question.

Sample Questions

  • Where is the wettest spot on earth?
  • How fast is the speed of light?
  • What is the meaning of life, the universe, and everything?

Notes

System performance is not optimal because the backend runs on Google Appengine's free tier. Most requests should take 30 seconds or less. Reload the page if request takes longer than 60 seconds.
